It has been a challenging ride working toward the finish line for Group 5. However, our becoming one by helping one
another enabled us to manage and reach the end of quarter 1 together. The process of how we found the need for
streaming was kind of easy as the subject's Experience Guides and the ideas of our members served an important role in
achieving our goal.


Though it may be easy to understand, it was hard to properly execute. We have focused on 3 certain content types
which are Educational Videos, Community Based Videos, and Video Podcasts. Forming these three choices were based on
what content type and platform would best suit our topic, online gaming addiction. In order to dig deeper on its viewers,
audience demographics were required so we released a survey. Releasing a survey was one of the most exciting part for
our group as it gives us the chance to communicate and understand viewers of a certain content type more, a crucial
step toward finding the need for streaming.


As a result, we are proud of the work we have done and will continue to work hard and do our best in the upcoming
tasks assigned to us, Group 5 members.
